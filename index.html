About This Property

Mountain Gem is a spaciously designed three-level log cabin offering two master suite bedrooms, two and a half bathrooms, a game room, and a theater room. Nestled in a natural wooded setting within a log cabin community, this retreat offers both mountain and wooded views and is just minutes from all the action of Pigeon Forge, Gatlinburg, and the Great Smoky Mountains National Park.

The main level features a cozy living room with gas fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops and stainless steel appliances, and a dining area with seating for four to six. A wall of windows and vaulted ceilings flood the space with natural light. The main level master suite offers a king bed and a full bathroom with tiled walk-in shower.

The upper level houses a second master suite with king bed and en-suite bath. The lower level is where all the action happens—a game room with pool table, Multicade, and pub-style table, plus a theater room with large TV, surround sound, reclining seats, and a queen sleeper sofa.

Outside, the main level deck features a covered screened hot tub with privacy louvers, a gas fireplace, TV, and seating for two. Free wired and wireless internet access and Smart TVs throughout make it easy to stream your favorites.

About This Property

On the crystal-clear, emerald-green waters of Lake Fontana, this four-bedroom lodge comfortably sleeps sixteen and comes with two large decks on multiple levels, a private dock with an upper deck and a boat slip. Bring your boat for the week!

With over 5,000 square feet of seamless indoor/outdoor living, this recently renovated and elegantly equipped lakefront home features three large living spaces and a chef's gourmet kitchen. Cathedral ceilings, a dramatic stacked stone wood-burning fireplace, and awe-inspiring views of Lake Fontana and the Smoky Mountains create a sanctuary for the adults in your party.

The gourmet kitchen features beautiful granite countertops and upgraded stainless steel appliances, including a professional Viking six-burner range with griddle and double convection ovens, a warming drawer, a professional oversized refrigerator and freezer, and a center island.

A game loft with pool table, poker table, and foosball table, plus a huge basement living area with a 70-inch smart TV, create fun-filled spaces for everyone. The basement opens to a large deck with an eight-person hot tub and outdoor dining.

Take the meandering trail through tall tree canopies to the private two-story boat dock where you can enjoy complimentary kayaks, stand-up paddleboards, and basic fishing poles. Bryson City, Deep Creek tubing, waterfall hikes, Tsali trails, Nantahala rafting, and zip-lining are all nearby.

About This Property

This listing includes both the main house and the guest house—7 bedrooms total across nearly 7,500 square feet of luxury living.

The Main House

Perched at the end of a peninsula in one of the premiere communities in the Great Smoky Mountains, this impressive estate offers wonderful lake views. Mountain elegance abounds in this 5-bedroom, 5.5-bathroom masterpiece, towering majestically above the clear blue waters of Fontana Lake. Professional designers and architects created this home in 2008, with huge mountain boulders creating the palette for nearly 7,500 square feet adorned with the finest quality finishes and decor.

The main floor features a huge great room with towering stone fireplaces both indoors and out. The adjacent chef's kitchen is completely outfitted with high-quality appliances from the Thermador range to the Miele espresso machine built into the formal dining room hutch. Also on the main floor: a wine room, half bath, and a king bedroom suite.

The lower level offers a slate billiard table, huge leather sectional home theater, large saltwater hot tub on the covered patio, foosball, a bunk room with 4 queen-sized bunks, and a second laundry room. Head upstairs (or take the convenient elevator) to find the expansive master suite, a second king master bedroom, a sitting room, and a complete gym.

The Guest House

Located above the 3-car garage, the guest house is appointed with the same luxury and decor as the main house. Enter through an outside entrance into a shared lobby with wet bar and coffee station. One king bedroom and one double-queen bedroom flank the lobby, sharing a beautifully appointed bathroom.
